onConfirm: instantiates with a copy of unleash, used for other abilities\
splash: holds splash sprite\
pierce: boolean\
slash: boolean\
targetlist: listm optional unless this is post targeting\
caster: player source of the damage\
statuses: struct of status flags (see below)\
selected: slotID of the center target\
source: menu that sent it, "djinni" "summon" "weapon" "item" "psynergy"\


healing: number of points for target to recover
healingratio: % of maxhp to heal
revive: boolean for if this targets dead players exclusively
